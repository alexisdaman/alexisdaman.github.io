The SnapSack is, at its most basic, a backpack.
What makes it special is that you won't have to think twice about preparing for a toke break— the SnapSack has everything you’ll need or want.

It has built–in speakers.
It was built–in wifi.

It comes with a glass pipe and papers.
You’ll never run out of fire because it comes with 5 lighters and a back–up pack of matches.

Do your eyes get red? The SnapSack comes with eyedrops.
Worried about smelling like stale smoke? The SnapSack sack comes with body and linen spray that smells like laundry. It also comes with gum to freshen your breath.

At SnapSack, we know how important it is to quench your thirst and feed your munchies— the SnapSack comes with 3 cans of Peace Tea and an assortment of snacks (some crunchy, some chewy, some salty, some sweet). It even comes with a thermal chamber so you can bring ice cream on the go (or keep your bong water ice cold).

If you’re out on an adventure, you’ll be happy to find all these things in your SnapSack...Plus, you’ll find all sorts of entertainment too— a hackysack, a frisbee (also makes a great rolling tray), pens and paper...

And, there’s still room for whatever else you want to bring along...room in the main chamber and the secret compartments.
